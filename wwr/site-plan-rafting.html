Overview

Purpose

Build revenue and provide safe, fun, and wholesome recreational activities for familes everywhere around the country. We hope to bring familes closer together and they learn how to work as a team to nativgate down the river.

Audience

Who are the target customers? -Familes with younger children who like to get out and do things together as a family. They enjoy the outdoors and have a decent income to do things together as a family. Families who haven't really gone before and others that go all the time What do they expect to find? -Find a team of indivuals who will take them white water rafting, taking care of all the equipment needs and guiding them down the river. A wholesome activity that will brind and unite the family closer together. What do they want? -A way to get out of the house for a weekend. An experience to bring them all closer together. A safe and fun time doing something that they either have done many times or have never done before.
